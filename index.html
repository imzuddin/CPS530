Bannana Bread

Ingredients

Ingredient Amount
All Pourpose Flour 1 Cup
Baking Soda 1/2 tsp
Salt 1/3 tsp
Brown Sugar 1 Cup
Butter 1/2 Cup
Eggs 2 Whole
Bannanas 3 Whole

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 172C and lightly grease a loaf pan
  2. Combine incredients in a large bowl. Combine using an electric mixer until smooth
  3. Pour mixture into pan and bake the loaf for about 20min
